About me

I've always had a passion for media. When I started working at the age of 16, I worked in a video rental store, and then I moved on to a cd shop where I became an assistant manager and section leader, which meant that I was in charge of a specific genre, which fuelled my passion further. I've always wanted to immerse myself in multimedia and I began to study multimedia design. I had learned basic C+ and advanced photoshop and premiere pro. all while learning how to focus my talent into something constructive. I had always wanted to get involved in making video games, and I thought that studying software engineering would lead me to my goal of running a successful multimedia company that covers music production, film, tv, animation and gaming.

For the past 8 years, I have worked as an international art dealer for one of the largest galleries in the world 'Park West Gallery', and had quickly worked my way up to Art Auctioneer and Gallery Director. The best part of this job, is not just being able to surround myself with some of the best artworks by some of the greatest artists in the world such as Pablo Picasso, Fransisco Goya, Rembrandt van Rijn and contemporary masters such as Romero Britto Alexandre Renoir, and Michael Cheval, but I also have the opportunity to travel on some of the worlds' most luxurious cruise ships and sharing my passion with guests from all over the world. 


This opportunity afforded me the chance to become a professional public speaker, and convey my knowledge to our guests in a manner that is informative and entertaining, which results in our guests collecting exquisite artworks.

I have always had a desire to learn new things and immerse myself completely into what I am doing. As I researched which language I should begin with, I realised that Software Engineering is not only an extroadinarily interesting field, but it's something that I can use my years of experience working with people to achieve a singular goal and vision.
